FreeCodeCamp Folsom is a group of new coders looking to improve apon their skills in all aspects of programming and coding. We meet so we can get to know eachother, help eachother in our goals to become better programmers and have fun during our quest.

Where We Meet

We are a community focused on working and meeting in the Folsom area. We currently try to meet at the Barns And Noble Coffee shop in the Palladio Shopping Center located off East Bidwell Street in Folsom. However, depending on our needs we may change locations at any time. On the left you can find a map and link to the menu at Barns and Nobles.
